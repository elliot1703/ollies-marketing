[ Straight Talk ]

Questions you're probably thinking

[ "I don't have time to record a podcast every week." ]

You don't need to. We batch-film 4 episodes in one morning — about 2 hours of your time per month. We handle everything else: scheduling, editing, posting, distribution. Your only job is to talk about what you already know.

[ "I'm not good on camera." ]

Nobody thinks they are at first. That's why we handle the direction, lighting, and framing. After 10 minutes in the chair, every client forgets the camera is there. The format is a conversation, not a performance — and that's exactly what your audience wants to see.

[ "Is video content actually worth the investment?" ]

The average Google Ads click costs $10-15 and disappears the moment you stop paying. Content you create exists permanently — it compounds. A single episode can generate traffic, leads, and credibility for years. At $2,500/month, you're investing less than a modest Google Ads budget and building an asset you own.

[ "What if I run out of things to talk about?" ]

You won't. Our strategy team plans your content calendar based on what your audience is searching for — rate updates, market commentary, client questions, industry trends. Most clients have a 12-month backlog of topics after our first planning session.
