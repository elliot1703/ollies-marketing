[ Common Questions ]

Before you book.

  • I already have a property photographer.

    Most agents do — for stills. We cover stills, drone, and social reels in one shoot with one team. No more juggling three freelancers. And the studio sessions for your personal brand? That's the part no photographer offers.

  • I'm not great on camera.

    That's exactly why the studio exists. Professional lighting, a relaxed setting, and a team that handles everything. We've filmed plenty of people who said the same thing — and they looked like naturals.

  • How fast do I get my deliverables?

    48 hours for standard turnaround. We know a hot market doesn't wait — your property media shouldn't either.

  • What if I just need one listing shot?

    The Engine package at $500 is built for exactly that. No commitment, no retainer. Book when you need it.

  • What about drone restrictions and weather?

    All drone flights are weather and airspace dependent — safety first, always. If conditions prohibit flying on shoot day, we'll reschedule the aerial component at no extra cost.

  • Are there any extra fees?

    Any required venue permits are billed at cost. Travel outside the standard Brisbane metro area may incur a small travel fee — always quoted before you commit.
